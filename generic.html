Biography

I was born in Vadodara, India and growing up I loved playing Cricket and working with technology. My parents and I moved from India to Tampa, Florida when I was seven years old. I attended both middle school and high school in Tampa. Currently, I’m a Senior at the University of South Florida majoring in Business Analytics and Information Systems.

My goal is to discover new ways technology and data can help make a positive impact on the society. I discovered this newfound interest last summer, when I did an Internship at AaNeel, a healthcare software company in which I fell in love with. This field has allowed me to step beyond the traditional boundaries and limits of my discipline and explore new territories.

My personal goal in life is to never stop learning and always seek discomfort. My favorite show of all time is Suits, and the main character Harvey Specter changed the way I look at the world. His quote “the only time success comes before work is in the dictionary” motivated me to work harder during my freshman year in college and that led me to be on the dean’s list. Another quote I live heavily by is “skills are cheap. passion is priceless” by Gary Vaynerchuck. I personally believe that whatever you do in life, must have a passion or otherwise it will be difficult to reach your full potential.
