Biography

My goal is to discover new ways technology can help make a positive impact on the healthcare industry. I discovered this newfound interest last summer, when I did an Internship at AaNeel, a healthcare software company in which I fell in love with. This field has allowed me to step beyond the traditional boundaries and limits of my discipline and explore new territories.

My personal goal in life is to never stop learning. In the future, I’d like to become an entrepreneur and run my own company. My favorite show of all time is Suits, and the main character Harvey Specter changed the way I look at the world. His quote “the only time success comes before work is in the dictionary” motivated me to work harder during my freshman year in college and that led me to be on the dean’s list. Another quote I live heavily by is “skills are cheap. passion is priceless” by Gary Vaynerchuck. I personally believe that whatever you do in life, must have a passion or otherwise it will be difficult to reach your full potential
